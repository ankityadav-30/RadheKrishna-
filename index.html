The Divine Union

One Soul, Two Bodies

Radha and Krishna are not merely historical figures; they represent the highest philosophy of love. Krishna is the energetic (Shaktiman), and Radha is the energy (Shakti).

Their love (Prema) exists beyond the material realm. In Vrindavan, every tree, every peacock, and every grain of sand is immersed in their eternal devotion.

To understand Krishna, you must understand Radha. She is the pleasure potency of the Divine.
